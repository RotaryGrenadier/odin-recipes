Nachos

Description

This recipe is a simple dish that can be completed within 30 minutes. If you love chips, meat, and cheese, you will satisfy your craving with this combo. You can choose a variety of protiens for the recipe, but for this example, we'll choose beef.

Ingredients

  • 1 Party-sized bag of tortilla chips
  • 1 Package of ground beef
  • 1 Packet of taco seasoning
  • 2 Bags of shredded mexican style cheese
  • (Optional) 1/2 Jar of spicy red salsa

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit
  2. Cook ground beef until browned
  3. Drain fat from ground beef and stir in taco seasoning
  4. Line baking sheet with aluminum foil
  5. Cover baking sheet with tortilla chips
  6. Layer chips with cheese
  7. Sprinkle cooked beef over cheese
  8. Layer more cheese over beef
  9. Place baking sheet in over
  10. Turn on broil after cheese is melted
  11. Take out of oven with cheese is browned
  12. (Optional)Cover with spicy salsa
  13. Split into equal portions, plate, and enjoy
